I am a PhD student in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Princeton University. My current research interests include:
  • experimental studies of high Reynolds number dynamic stall and its effects on vertical axis wind turbines
  • studies of the future of offshore wind energy using an integrated assessment model
  • field studies of turbulence in the atmospheric surface layer
